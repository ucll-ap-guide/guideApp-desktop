Mobile GuidO guide

Start Menu

On the start menu, you have the following options:

  1. Map overview

    Here you have a list of all available maps you can open. To open a map just click on the name.

  2. Download or delete a map

    Click on the download symbol to download a map to your device. This way you can use it while being offline. To delete a map from your device, click on the garbage bin symbol.

  3. Map search

    You can search for a specific map by typing its name in the search box.

Map Viewer

When you have selected a map, you will be taken to this screen. Here you have a 3D view of the building.

General

  1. Current floor

    This is the floor you are currently looking at.

  2. Information

    Press on the “i” symbol to show the description of a selected room.

  3. Hamburger

    Press the hamburger menu to show or hide the extra options.

  4. Arrow up

    Go to the floor above the current floor.

  5. Arrow down

    Go to the floor beneath the current floor.

  6. Perspective slider

    With this slider you can change the camera perspective to look from above or have a close-up 3D view.

  7. Center camera

    Set the camera to the center of the current floor.

  8. Points of interest

    Select which points of interest are visible.

  9. Settings

    Adjust your settings.

  10. Home

    Go back to the Start Menu.

  11. Search path

    Press the magnifying glass symbol to search for a path.

Points of interest

Points of interest are locations you might find interesting or useful.

In the Points Of Interest Menu you can toggle the visibility of individual or all points of interest. When the check mark symbol is visible, the point of interest is visible.

Changes made are applied automatically.

Settings

Top View Camera

  1. Swipe sensitivity

    This changes the responsiveness of swiping on the screen. Slide to the left to lower the sensitivity and slide to the right to increase the sensitivity.

First Person Camera

  1. Enable gyroscope

    Check this box to enable the use of the gyroscope. The gyroscope tracks where your phone is looking at in the real world and translates this to the virtual world.

  2. Gyroscope sensitivity

    This changes the responsiveness of the gyroscope. Slide to the left to lower the sensitivity and slide to the right to increase the sensitivity.

  3. Swipe sensitivity

    This changes the responsiveness of swiping on the screen. Slide to the left to lower the sensitivity and slide to the right to increase the sensitivity.

Don’t forget to press the save symbol to save your changes. This way the settings will be remembered even after closing the app.

Pathfinding

Select your starting point and destination and find the shortest path.

  1. From

    Enter your starting point by typing or choosing it from the dropdown menu.

  2. To

    Enter your destination by typing or choosing it from the dropdown menu.

  3. QR code scanner

    If the room you are in has a room QR code, you can scan it to select your current position as a starting point.

  4. Navigate

    Press the “Navigate” button to search the shortest path.

    5. After setting a path, you can choose to follow the route in first person mode.

  5. Camera

    Enter first person mode by pressing the “Camera” icon.

First person mode

Select your starting point and destination and find the shortest path.

In first person mode you can look around by swiping and the gyroscope.

You can move forward and backward on the path by swiping down and up respectively.

  1. Map

    Go back to Top View.

  2. Arrow

    Indicates forward direction and sets your view forward when clicked.
